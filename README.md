# ai-policy-tracker
An automated, open-source tracker monitoring AI governance 
legislation and policy developments across the UK, EU, and US.
Updated daily. Plain-English summaries generated for every entry.
## Why This Exists
AI policy is moving fast but existing trackers are aimed at 
lawyers, updated manually, and paywalled. This tool is free, 
automated, and written for researchers and the public.
## What It Tracks
- 🇬🇧 UK: GOV.UK AI consultations, DSIT, AI Safety Institute
- 🇪🇺 EU: EU AI Act implementation updates
- 🇺🇸 US: AI-related bills in Congress
